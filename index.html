About Us

At Harmony, we bring together creativity, craftsmanship, and artistry to offer unique, handmade treasures for your home. What started as a personal passion for pottery and handcrafts in a small home studio has grown into a celebration of beautiful, thoughtfully made pieces. From hand-thrown ceramics to artfully designed candles and original paintings, every item is crafted with care and intention.


Our mission is to create high-quality, one-of-a-kind pieces that add warmth, beauty, and inspiration to your space. By choosing handmade, you’re not only supporting artisans but also bringing home something special, crafted to be cherished for years to come.


Discover the beauty of handmade at Harmony and let us help you fill your home with unique, meaningful creations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of products does Harmony offer?
We offer a range of handcrafted items, including ceramics, candles, and original paintings. Each piece is carefully designed and made by artisans, ensuring it is one-of-a-kind.
Are all products handmade?
Yes! Every item at Harmony is handmade with care, from our pottery to our candles and artwork. We believe in the beauty of craftsmanship and the uniqueness of each handmade piece.
Do you accept custom orders?
Absolutely! If you're looking for a personalized ceramic piece, custom candle scents, or a unique artwork, feel free to contact us. We love bringing your creative ideas to life.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary depending on your location, but most orders are processed and shipped within 3-5 business days. We’ll provide tracking information as soon as your order is on its way.
What if I receive a damaged item?
In the rare case that your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving it. We’ll work with you to resolve the issue, either through a replacement or a refund.
